Ironside Team History

Boston Ironside is a world-renowned ultimate frisbee team based in Boston, MA.

Ironside was formed from the merger of Death or Glory, a record-setting team based in Boston that earned 6 consecutive national championships in the '90s, and Twisted Metal, an upstart crew of young guns from Cambridge. After a transitional year in 2007, Boston Ironside was officially born in 2008.

From 2008-2017, Boston Ironside achieved significant success on the national and world stage. The team placed no lower than 5th at the US National Championships during these years, and earned a record 9 consecutive semi-finals berths from 2008-2016. Ironside advanced to the national championship game five times (2008, 2010, 2011, 2014, 2016), and brought the championship back to Boston in 2016.

Boston Ironside has represented the United States at international competitions in Prague, Czech Republic, and Lecco, Italy. The roster has also boasted five different players who have earned the prestigious Callahan Award as the best player in college ultimate.
